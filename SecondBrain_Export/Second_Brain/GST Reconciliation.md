# GST Reconciliation

## Details
Ingests purchase invoices (PDF/image), extracts GSTIN, invoice number, taxable amount, and GST amount, cross-references against GSTR-2A data from the GST portal, and flags mismatches instantly. It saves businesses actual tax money by automating the painful and error-prone process of reconciling invoices.
